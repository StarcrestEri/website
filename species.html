Other Species

Neumin - Descendants of humanity adapted to the Surya System. Slender with elongated ears, they store bioatomic energy derived from quasi-quartz. A unique trait: their skin can radiate heat rapidly to cool their bodies during energy discharge.

Kero - Amphibious beings from the icy world Himavat. They breathe both water and air and possess webbed limbs. A distinctive feature is chlorophyll-like cells in their skin allowing minimal photosynthesis.

Merfolk - Aquatic humanoids of Yamuna with fins and gills. Their bodies regenerate severed fins quickly. They also have electro-sensory organs along their lateral line.

Faefolk - Winged, light-framed people associated with Indra's stormbands. Their feathers shift color with air pressure, enabling limited atmospheric manipulation.

Scorpionfolk - Predatory, chitinous humanoids of Vishnu. Their tails deliver venom and their exoskeleton is naturally magnetic, aiding in desert navigation.

Laima - Desert-dwelling humans of Vishnu with tough skin and enhanced sunlight vision. They are adept at crafting and survival in arid conditions.

Vortex - Phantasmal entities from the Dream Land linked to the black hole. They can phase through solid matter and emit disorienting energy pulses.
