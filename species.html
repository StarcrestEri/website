Other Species

Kero - An amphibious race native to the icy planet Himavat. Around 50 BNE many migrated to the swampy moon Tara to escape the harsh cold, establishing a new society. They value community and simple algae-based technologies; their culture is matrilineal and spiritual, with rituals tied to the moon's phases.

Merfolk - Inhabitants of the ocean world Yamuna whose cities grew around decaying oil rigs from a pre-Empire age. The Niyo regime hunted and enslaved numerous merfolk until Alia's rescue of Aila sparked a brief uprising.

Faefolk - Winged, graceful beings of the gas giant Indra. Luna is one such individual. They inhabit floating cities above the planet's stormbands and possess an affinity for wind and lightning. Their society is insular, often mistrustful of outsiders, though rebels sometimes earn their allegiance.

Scorpionfolk - A predatory, barbed-tailed species on Vishnu that extorted the Laima for parts and services. They are militaristic and feared by desert populations.

Laima - A desert people of Vishnu. Humble and tight-knit, they live near small repair outposts and resent the Scorpionfolk's exploitation. Their culture values hospitality and survival.
