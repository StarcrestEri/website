Other Species

Kero – An amphibious race native to the icy planet Himavat. In 50 BNE many migrated to the swampy moon Tara, where they built a new society.

Merfolk – Inhabitants of the ocean world Yamuna. They live among sunken oil rigs and were persecuted by the Niyo Empire.

Faefolk – Winged, graceful beings seen on the gas giant Indra. Luna is one such individual.
