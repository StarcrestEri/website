History

Long before the current era, humanity fled a universe dominated by antimatter. Their sun died and Earth became a tomb. Survivors crossed the void and settled in a star system within the Andromeda Galaxy they called Surya.

Over thousands of years new forms emerged. By 4000 BNE a new branch of humanity known as the Neumin had evolved; they were slender, quick, and capable of storing bioatomic energy. Their physiology depended on rare Quasi-Quartz crystals. Artificial incubation replaced traditional birth beginning 3500 BNE and by 2000 BNE most Neumin were born sexless, though cultural gendering rituals persisted.

In 1500 BNE the first exosuits were built, granting Neumin greater power at the cost of mobility. A catastrophic accident at an incubation facility in 500 BNE prompted stricter regulation of those centres.

Near the end of the Before New Era, 50 BNE saw the amphibious Kero arrive on the moon Tara. Over the next decades important citizens were born: Rina, her children Sami, Alia and Asha, and the astronomer Niyo. Niyos curiosity with a mysterious black hole shaped the subsequent millennia. By 8 BNE work began on a colossal energy-harvesting ring whose completion and later expansion would define the Niyo Empires ambition.

The post-New Era brought political upheaval. Niyos descent into madness in 53 BNE, the disappearance of Sami into what was later called Shiva, and the promise of resurrection bound Rina to Niyos service. The 1 NE coup effectively erased the Neumin Empire, replacing it with an authoritarian regime that exploited exiles and advanced weaponized suits. Cultural memory of the Neumin Empire persists only in outlawed artifacts and whispered histories.

By 5 BNE Niyo had succumbed to the radiation and madness of the black hole. When Sami was lost inside the growing singularity in 3 BNE, Niyo used the tragedy to bind Rina to her service. Niyo seized control in 1 NE, toppling the Neumin Empire and founding the Niyo Empire. Those deemed undesirable were exiled to the ice world Himavat in 2 NE to construct Shiva, a mechaplanet shell around the black hole. By 6 NE the ring had become a world and the empire’s tyranny was complete.
