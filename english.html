Alia and the Lost Stars

One night, young Alia went to bed wishing for a good night's sleep...

But she fell into a deep coma and wandered into the Dream Land.

While traveling through the Dream Land, she meets Saria, a girl who has been trapped in dreams forever.

To return to her own world, she must collect the scattered stars, which represent her memories.

If she fails to collect all the stars, she will be trapped in the Dream Land forever.

Vortex are from the Dream Land, and at the end awaits the Vortex Queen.

Be careful of traps in the Dream Land.
