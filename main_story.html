Main Story Summary

6 NE Present – Alia flies from Sita to Tara, fights Havoc and damages a ship, then barely escapes. She returns to Sita and is ordered to repair the ship on the Eclipse but instead heads to Vishnu. There she battles a red-black armored woman and frees the Laima from Scorpionfolk control. She then crash-lands on Yamuna, rescues the mer named Aila, and again defeats Havoc on an oil rig.

Later Alia refits her ship and travels to Indra, helping defend the sky cities from a mechanical wyvern and once more confronting Havoc. She then ventures to Agni, battles in a volcanic landscape, and discovers the armored woman is her mother, Rina, who aligned with the Niyo Empire.

The conflict between Alia and her mother underscores the larger struggle against the empire. Havoc continues to pursue her across worlds while she aids oppressed peoples.
