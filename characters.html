Characters

Alia – Protagonist; trained fighter, daughter of Rina, opposed to the Niyo Empire.

Rina – Alia’s mother; strict commander, later revealed to be allied with the empire.

Asha – Alia’s younger sister; gentle and watchful.

Sami – Alia’s sibling who died in the black hole Shiva.

Niyo – Astronomer turned mad emperor; architect of the Niyo Empire and the Shiva project.

Havoc – Imperial commander; repeatedly battles Alia.

Aila – Merfolk rescued by Alia on Yamuna.

Luna – Faefolk from Indra who calls for Alia’s aid.

Laima – Desert people on Vishnu helped by Alia.
