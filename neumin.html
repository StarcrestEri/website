Neumin

Neumin are the descendants of humanity who adapted to life in the Surya System. They are slender, fast, and possess elongated ears. A bioatomic adaptation allows them to store trace amounts of energy from certain chemicals. To amplify this ability they rely on Quasi-Quartz crystals or injectable liquid serum.

Sexual reproduction was abandoned millennia ago. By 3500 BNE mass artificial incubation became the norm, and by 2000 BNE most Neumin were born sexless. Society still maintains gender rituals at birth and informal norms, but sexed individuals are extremely rare.

Their bodies undergo a monthly "Silicacrine Cycle" to purge residual Quasi-Quartz particles. A specialized organ, the Quasisilicaureal gland in the inner ear, handles this metabolism and is central to several medical conditions.

To channel their bioatomic power in combat Neumin developed exosuits. Early models were bulky and restrictive; later generations became lighter and more agile.
