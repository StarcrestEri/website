Niyo Empire

The Niyo Empire replaced the earlier Neumin Empire in 1 NE when the astronomer Niyo seized power. Fuelled by hatred campaigns, the new regime oppressed those labeled "undesirable." Many were exiled to Himavat to build the Shiva mechaplanet, a shell surrounding the black hole that drained cosmic energy.

Order was enforced by commanders such as Havoc. Niyo herself oversaw construction of the energy ring and later the expanded world. Imperial technology included agile bioatomic suits and massive war machines like mechanical wyverns.

The empire’s tyranny reached every world in the Surya system, incorporating forced labour, exile and a militarized hierarchy. Its fall has yet to come.
