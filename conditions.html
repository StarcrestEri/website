Medical Conditions

Quasisilicatonia – A balanced metabolic state with normal quasi-quartz regulation.

Quasisilicaneplasia – Genetic absence of functional quasi-quartz receptors or metabolism.

Quasisilicadysria – Unstable or inconsistent quasi-quartz metabolism.

Quasisilicatropism – Hyper-efficient resonance allowing passive energy absorption (quasisilicaptosis).

Quasisilicacarcinergy – Hypersensitive immune response to quasi-quartz causing allergic reactions.

Quasisilicaureitis – Autoimmune attack on the Quasisilicaureal gland causing fatigue, dizziness, and metabolic crashes.

QARS (Quasisilica Auto-rejection Syndrome) – Severe immunometabolic disorder where the body rejects quasi-quartz, attacking implants and internal stores.

QDLS (Quasisilicadermal Load Syndrome) – Dermal condition where excess quasi-quartz accumulates in skin pores, causing painful lesions.
