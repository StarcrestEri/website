Privacy Policy

________________________________________

This Privacy Policy describes how Piyoverse Entertainment handles information in relation to the video game collection currently released as "Starcrest" and planned to be released in the future as "Starcrest Saga".

________________________________________

About the Game

Starcrest is a collection of emulated video games developed and published by
Piyoverse Entertainment. The collection includes the titles "Alia and the Lost
Stars" (SG-1000), "Starcrest" (Sega Mark III), and "Starcrest 2"
(Sega Mega Drive).

________________________________________

Information Collection

Piyoverse Entertainment does not collect, store, transmit, or process any
personal information from users through the Starcrest game collection.

The software operates entirely offline and does not require an internet
connection. The game does not include analytics, tracking systems,
advertising systems, online accounts, or network communication features.

________________________________________

Local Data

The game may store data locally on the user's device for gameplay purposes,
such as save data or configuration settings. This information remains on
the user's device and is not transmitted to Piyoverse Entertainment.

________________________________________

Third-Party Services

Starcrest does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising,
or online service providers within the software itself.

Distribution platforms such as Steam may independently collect information
according to their own policies. Piyoverse Entertainment does not control
or receive that information.

________________________________________

Changes to This Policy

Piyoverse Entertainment may update this Privacy Policy if the software
changes in a way that affects user privacy. Any updated version will be
provided with the game or through official distribution channels.

________________________________________

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact
Piyoverse Entertainment through its official distribution pages.
For general inquiries, please email: contact@piyoverse.net
