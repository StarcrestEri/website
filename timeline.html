Timeline of the Surya System

  • 8000 BNE – Humanity flees antimatter universe and settles in the Surya Star System in Andromeda.
  • 4000 BNE – Evolution produces the Neumin; long ears, bioatomic energy storage, Quasi-Quartz reliance.
  • 3500 BNE – Mass artificial incubation begins; sexual organs rendered useless.
  • 3000 BNE – Neumin births become sexless majority.
  • 2000 BNE – Sexless Neumin are the majority; gender rituals persist.
  • 1500 BNE – Cumbersome exosuits developed to augment bioatomic powers.
  • 500 BNE – Devastating incubation facility accident; stricter legislation follows.
  • 50 BNE – Amphibious Kero migrate from Himavat to Tara, moon of Sita.
  • 40–12 BNE – Key figures born: Rina, Sami, Alia, Asha; Niyo studies a forming black hole and designs agile suits.
  • 8 BNE – Construction of an energy-harvesting ring around the black hole begins.
  • 5–3 BNE – Niyo descends into madness; Sami disappears in the black hole later called Shiva.
  • 1 NE – Niyo seizes power; Neumin Empire replaced by Niyo Empire with oppression.
  • 2 NE – Undesirables exiled to Himavat to build components for Shiva mechaplanet.
  • 6 NE – Present day; the ring has become a world, and tyranny reigns.
