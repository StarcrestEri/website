Locations

Agni - A volcanic world of rivers of magma; source of quasi-quartz mining and the site where Alia confronted Rina.

Vishnu - A desert planet of rust-coloured sands; repair station of the Laima and territory of the Scorpionfolk.

Sita - A central Neumin world, home to villages like Mesi and the orbital cruiser Eclipse.

Tara - A lush swampy moon of Sita inhabited by the amphibious Kero.

Asteroid Belt - A dense region of rocks encircling the system; Yamuna lies deep within it.

Yamuna - An ocean world within the belt, featuring ancient oil rigs and merfolk.

Indra - A gas giant outside the belt with floating sky cities and stormbands; home of Luna and faefolk.

Himavat - An icy world used as exile labor camps for undesirables building Shiva.

Shiva - A mechaplanet formed from a black hole ring, the Niyo Empire's ultimate structure.
