Locations

Surya Star System – The system in Andromeda where humanity resettled.

Sita – A planet that serves as a central world for the Neumin; home to villages such as Mesi and the orbital cruiser Eclipse.

Himavat – An icy world where undesirables were exiled to mine and build components for Shiva.

Tara – A lush swampy moon of Sita inhabited by the amphibious Kero.

Shiva – Originally an energy-harvesting ring around a black hole, later transformed into a mechaplanet by the Niyo Empire.

Vishnu – A desert planet of rust-coloured sands; location of the Laima repair station and encounters with Scorpionfolk.

Yamuna – An ocean world with ancient oil rigs and merfolk such as Aila.

Indra – A gas giant with floating sky cities and stormbands; home of Luna and faefolk.

Agni – A volcanic world of rivers of magma; where Alia confronts her mother Rina.
